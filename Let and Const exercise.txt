1. What is the difference between var and let?
	var = reassign and redeclare, but you cannot redeclare with let. You can also access a hoisted variable with var while let creates a block scope.
2. What is the difference between var and const?
	var = reassign and redeclare, but you cannot redeclare or reassign with const. Const creates block scope.
3. What is the difference between let and const?
	Reassign with let, cannot redeclare or reassign with const.
4. What is hoisting?
	Variables are able to be "lifted" or "hoisted to the top of the scope they are declared in. Function declares are also hoisted and can be included "before" they are defined.
